About Us

Family owned and operated, Pantheon Pizza is a comfortable and inviting spot for casual dinning and homemade authentic italian cuisine. The unfussy atmoshpere makes this a simple and quick choice for pizza and other favorites. Delivery and takeout services also provide conveniet options. Situated close to the International Speedway, area beaches, and Daytona State College, Pantheon Pizza draws guests of all ages and tastes. In addition to the always popular pizza selections, the menu features various incarnations of strombolis, calzones, seafood dinners, hot oven grinders, and italian classics like stuffed shells, lasagna and chicken parmigiana. There's a modest array of beverages available, including bottled beers and wines, and a handful of desserts such as baklava and cannolis which can make a nice addition to any order.
